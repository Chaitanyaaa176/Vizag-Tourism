The City of Destiny

Visakhapatnam also known as “Vizag” is the administrative headquarters of Visakhapatnam district in Andhra Pradesh. Vizag also known as the “City of Destiny” in India is one of oldest and richest in terms of its being, culture and heritage.Visakhapatnam is a port city and industrial center in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, on the Bay of Bengal. It's known for its many beaches, hill stations and waterfalls.
